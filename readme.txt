== Description ==

Bring Stories to your Website. AMP Stories load quickly and create engaging experiences on your Wordpress site. They're also great for SEO and you can monetize them! With our simple drag and drop interface, you can start creating engaging AMP Stories in minutes.

Choose to embed your AMP Story as a full page or embed it anywhere on a page. Our goal is to help you do more with the fastest growing format of all time.

Create your AMP Stories from 1M+ images, videos, gifs, emojis, backgrounds, and templates. Our Team is working around the clock to give you the best experience with AMP Stories and we're excited for the opportunities this format offers.

By adding AMP Stories to your Website you can:
- Send traffic from social Stories to your AMP Story page
- Use an AMP Story as a Post or Page
- Add AMP Stories within your pages or content
- and so much more...

We look forward to working with you!

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g.

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/ztorie` directory, or install the plugin through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Use Ztorie page on sidebar to add Ztorie App key (you can get one at your team settings https://app.ztorie.com/settings/team )
